<div class="blog__post-content html-content">
<span id="hs_cos_wrapper_post_body" class="hs_cos_wrapper hs_cos_wrapper_meta_field hs_cos_wrapper_type_rich_text" style="" data-hs-cos-general-type="meta_field" data-hs-cos-type="rich_text"><p><img src="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg?width=1800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg" alt="forging_the_shadowhammer-1" width="1800" style="width: 1800px;" srcset="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg?width=900&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg 900w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg?width=1800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg 1800w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg?width=2700&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg 2700w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg?width=3600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg 3600w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg?width=4500&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg 4500w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg?width=5400&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer-1.jpg 5400w" sizes="(max-width: 1800px) 100vw, 1800px"></p>
<p>Operation <a href="https://securelist.com/operation-shadowhammer/89992/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">ShadowHammer</a> is a new and highly targeted supply chain attack discovered by Kaspersky Lab. The attack leveraged <a href="https://www.asus.com/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">ASUS</a> Live Update software to distribute malicious code. Live Update is a utility which commonly comes pre-installed on most ASUS computers and is used to update system drivers and BIOS/UEFI code.</p>
<!--more--><p><br>As the details of this supply chain attack are still unfolding our ReversingLabs researchers looked into what is currently publicly available. Using our Titanium Platform we’ve been able to make a few connections which lead us to, what we believe is, the first iteration of this malware code.</p>
<p>We started from the infected installation package that was published by Kaspersky Lab. Its content is the main installation file called Setup.exe and two additional MSI installation packages that contain all the software components that get installed on the system.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><br><img src="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png" alt="Forging the ShadowHammer" width="1600" style="width: 1600px;" srcset="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png?width=800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png 800w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png 1600w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png?width=2400&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png 2400w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png?width=3200&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png 3200w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png?width=4000&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png 4000w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png?width=4800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_01.png 4800w" sizes="(max-width: 1600px) 100vw, 1600px"> <span style="font-size: 14px;"><strong>Infected ASUS installation package - courtesy of Kaspersky Labs</strong></span></p>
<p><br>The main executable file, Setup.exe, carries the malicious payload. Because of this, we decided to take a look at how we could pivot around Setup.exe and find additional samples to analyze. Using our <a href="https://www.reversinglabs.com/technology/reversinglabs-hash-algorithm" rel="noopener" target="_blank">RHA1 </a>functional similarity algorithm we’ve been able to do just that and find 10 additional samples worth taking a closer look at.<br><br></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png" alt="Forging the ShadowHammer" width="1600" style="width: 1600px;" srcset="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png?width=800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png 800w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png 1600w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png?width=2400&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png 2400w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png?width=3200&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png 3200w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png?width=4000&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png 4000w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png?width=4800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_02.png 4800w" sizes="(max-width: 1600px) 100vw, 1600px"><br><span style="font-size: 14px;"><strong>Pivoting to find similar files via ReversingLabs RHA1 functional similarity algorithm</strong></span></p>
<p><br>From this point, the investigation is carried forward by <a href="https://www.reversinglabs.com/technology/malware-analysis-solution" rel="noopener" target="_blank">TitaniumCore</a>, our advanced file decomposition engine. TitaniumCore has been able to extract embedded resources from all of the RHA1 detected installation packages. The installation packages which have 58 extracted files are particularly interesting as they contain one variant of the ShadowHammer attack.<br><br></p>
<p><img src="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png" alt="Forging the ShadowHammer" width="1600" style="width: 1600px;" srcset="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png?width=800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png 800w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png 1600w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png?width=2400&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png 2400w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png?width=3200&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png 3200w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png?width=4000&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png 4000w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png?width=4800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_03.png 4800w" sizes="(max-width: 1600px) 100vw, 1600px"></p>
<p style="text-align: center; font-size: 14px;"><strong>Extracted resources via ReversingLabs TitaniumCore static unpacking engine</strong></p>
<p><br>This extracted executable is a Visual Studio C++ application that has been compiled with debug symbol information enabled. These symbols unveil a bit more about the attacker and the attack timeline.<br><br></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png" alt="Forging the ShadowHammer" width="1600" style="width: 1600px;" srcset="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png?width=800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png 800w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png 1600w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png?width=2400&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png 2400w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png?width=3200&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png 3200w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png?width=4000&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png 4000w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png?width=4800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_04.png 4800w" sizes="(max-width: 1600px) 100vw, 1600px"><span style="font-size: 14px;"><strong>PDB debugging information found within the extracted executable file</strong></span></p>
<p><br>Based on the specifics of the file path, we were able to conclude that this is the original code the adversaries developed specifically to carry out this attack. Since PDB paths are indexed by our advanced search capabilities, finding all the other samples that share this path, requires only a simple one keyword search.<br><br></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png" alt="Forging the ShadowHammer" width="1600" style="width: 1600px;" srcset="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png?width=800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png 800w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png 1600w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png?width=2400&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png 2400w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png?width=3200&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png 3200w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png?width=4000&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png 4000w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png?width=4800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_05.png 4800w" sizes="(max-width: 1600px) 100vw, 1600px"><br><strong><span style="font-size: 14px;">Pivoting to other versions of the same malware via ReversingLabs Advanced Search</span></strong></p>
<p><br>Within this batch of samples, we looked at the oldest one, which has the following debugging information.<br><br></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png" alt="Forging the ShadowHammer" width="1600" style="width: 1600px;" srcset="https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png?width=800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png 800w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png?width=1600&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png 1600w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png?width=2400&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png 2400w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png?width=3200&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png 3200w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png?width=4000&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png 4000w, https://www.reversinglabs.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Imported_Blog_Media/Forging%20the%20ShadowHammer/forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png?width=4800&amp;name=forging_the_shadowhammer_06.png 4800w" sizes="(max-width: 1600px) 100vw, 1600px"><strong><span style="font-size: 14px;">PDB debugging information found within the extracted executable file</span></strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The timestamp information within aligns perfectly with the attack timeline described by Kaspersky Lab.</p>
<p>The details of the attack are still being investigated by our own team and the teams of security researchers around the world. Our hope is that this short threat hunting blog will help those looking for more details as they put the pieces of this puzzle back together.</p>
