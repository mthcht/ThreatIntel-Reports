<li>Provides instant notifications via email or app and configurable, sharable reports for stakeholders.</li>

<p>Nagios has a steep learning curve, but it is very customizable. The open-source version is free to use, so many organizations use it.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Nagios has a large community of users and developers that produce addons and plugins to extend its functionality. It is considered one of the industry standards for monitoring purposes.&nbsp;</p>



<p><strong>Features</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Monitors servers, network devices, applications, services, and system metrics across the entire IT environment for performance and availability.</li>



<li>Sends notifications via email, SMS, or other methods when problems or thresholds are detected, with support for notification escalations to ensure timely response.</li>



<li>Supports thousands of official and community-developed plugins to expand monitoring for additional devices, protocols, and custom checks.</li>



<li>Provides a real-time graphical interface for viewing host and service statuses, trends, reports, and the overall health of your monitored environment.</li>



<li>Efficient, open-source engine scales to thousands of devices and services, with flexible configuration and automation options for diverse monitoring needs.</li>
</ul>
