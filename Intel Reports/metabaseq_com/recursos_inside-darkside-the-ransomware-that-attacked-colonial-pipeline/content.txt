					<style>.blog-text > h3 {color: #693840;font-family:"NeueMachina-Regular", Sans-serif;}.blog-text > h2 {color: #E82F49;font-family:"NeueMachina-Regular", Sans-serif;}.blog-text > img{width: 100%}ul {list-style: none;}.blog-text ul li::before {content: "\2022";color: #E82F49;font-weight: bold;display: inline-block;width: 1em; margin-left: -1em;}figure > div {width: 100%;}td {padding: 5px}figure figcaption {text-align: center;}</style>
<div class="blog-text">
<p><strong><span style="color: #e82f49;">By Miguel Gonzalez &amp; Jesus Dominguez from Metabase Q&#8217;s Ocelot Team</span></strong></p>
<h2 class="blog-text" data-w-id="6f5fa7e5-04f1-9a4c-e29e-440fdd33b459" data-wf-id="[&quot;6f5fa7e5-04f1-9a4c-e29e-440fdd33b459&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">Executive Summary</h2>
<p data-w-id="5d372993-45e4-2946-7ad8-114760cbef88" data-wf-id="[&quot;5d372993-45e4-2946-7ad8-114760cbef88&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">On May 7th, 2021, Colonial Pipeline reported that its digital infrastructure had been compromised due to a cyberattack, and as a precautionary measure, it would suspend its services until the severity of the situation was determined.</p>
<p data-w-id="d8937b02-fafe-e61b-ff5e-847c1514cb1a" data-wf-id="[&quot;d8937b02-fafe-e61b-ff5e-847c1514cb1a&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">Colonial is the largest pipeline operator in the U.S. and transports more than 3 million barrels of gasoline, diesel, and jet fuel between the U.S. Gulf Coast and the New York Harbor area.</p>
<p data-w-id="444c073e-d8e4-9147-703a-dc1b4bbfe1aa" data-wf-id="[&quot;444c073e-d8e4-9147-703a-dc1b4bbfe1aa&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">This cyberattack utilized ransomware which encrypted critical information systems and requested payment to recover the information.</p>
<p data-w-id="c011903f-18c3-6cc3-f021-49ca1b401bb2" data-wf-id="[&quot;c011903f-18c3-6cc3-f021-49ca1b401bb2&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">Researchers have allegedly attributed this attack to the DarkSide group, whose modus operandi known as Ransomware-as-a-Service (RaaS), involves not only the encryption of information with Salsa20, using an RSA-1024 public key, but also the exfiltration of information used for payment negotiation.</p>
<p data-w-id="3c982b7e-f0c4-0775-bd9e-1615b670da90" data-wf-id="[&quot;3c982b7e-f0c4-0775-bd9e-1615b670da90&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">DarkSide has affected numerous organizations in various sectors, including industry, legal, insurance, healthcare, and energy. However, according to a January 27th, 2021 publication on DarkSide’s website about its rules of use, affiliated individuals cannot target the funeral service sector, hospitals, nursing homes, or companies distributing the COVID-19 vaccine.</p>
<p data-w-id="502fe44a-c2a9-7410-fb75-ed61289b2b30" data-wf-id="[&quot;502fe44a-c2a9-7410-fb75-ed61289b2b30&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">To pay the demands, the organization promotes the use of Monero, a cryptocurrency distinguished by its anonymity and superior security compared to other currencies in the market. DarkSide members use an administrative panel via The Onion Router (TOR) to communicate with victims and manage the delivery of the ransomware to its victims.</p>
<p data-w-id="496991b5-de6e-7513-7998-870dd518cfee" data-wf-id="[&quot;496991b5-de6e-7513-7998-870dd518cfee&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">Government entities have already identified this specific version of the ransomware, and you can find the Indicators of Compromise (IOC) at the end of the blog.</p>
<p data-w-id="fbddaf97-883f-21c0-dafa-680ea99c570c" data-wf-id="[&quot;fbddaf97-883f-21c0-dafa-680ea99c570c&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">Colonial has already faced a series of extortions so that the stolen information is not published, and it is presumed that the payment has already been made for an amount close to $5 million USD, reported <a href="https://www.zdnet.com/article/colonial-pipeline-paid-close-to-5-million-in-ransomware-blackmail-payment/" data-w-id="abcbc549-e898-5a36-2aed-e58d7874154e" data-wf-id="[&quot;abcbc549-e898-5a36-2aed-e58d7874154e&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">zdnet</a>. According to researcher <a href="https://krebsonsecurity.com/2021/05/darkside-ransomware-gang-quits-after-servers-bitcoin-stash-seized/" data-w-id="ad3a53e4-af00-de9b-ce8a-5eec436551a4" data-wf-id="[&quot;ad3a53e4-af00-de9b-ce8a-5eec436551a4&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">Brian Krebs</a>, the group’s servers have already been seized, as well as one of the cryptocurrency accounts used to pay its affiliates.</p>
<p data-w-id="dad5f835-2393-f83a-506a-a567e5d19951" data-wf-id="[&quot;dad5f835-2393-f83a-506a-a567e5d19951&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">In this blog, we analyze in detail one of the DarkSide samples used during the attack, providing TTPs (Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures) and IOCs (Indicators of Compromise) that serve for monitoring, detection, and eradication strategies of this type of cyberthreats.</p>
<h2 class="blog-text" data-w-id="149858f3-d83f-6885-394a-36d814117a7d" data-wf-id="[&quot;149858f3-d83f-6885-394a-36d814117a7d&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">Main findings</h2>

<h2 class="blog-text" data-w-id="5945d1c8-7aaa-952c-7e9b-9ac69f91b732" data-wf-id="[&quot;5945d1c8-7aaa-952c-7e9b-9ac69f91b732&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">Initial infection</h2>
<p data-w-id="8394ed20-3ba5-464a-a5d1-db9469012ff5" data-wf-id="[&quot;8394ed20-3ba5-464a-a5d1-db9469012ff5&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">Malicious actors use techniques such as spear-phishing with malicious links to try to infect their victims. In turn, they obtain legitimate credentials through SQL Injection attacks by accessing credentials stored in databases or even in different places on the dark web where compromised credentials of organizations are listed. According to Mandiant, in the case of Colonial, the attackers used valid VPN credentials of an employee who did not have multi-factor authentication enabled, and remote access was achieved without restrictions.</p>

<p data-w-id="abec9d05-e997-46e9-16a7-a9c5b892a090" data-wf-id="[&quot;abec9d05-e997-46e9-16a7-a9c5b892a090&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">Ransomware attacks have become the top method used by attackers worldwide to obtain large amounts of money in a short period of time. ransomware payments have tripled from 2019 to date. In the case of Colonial, due to the critical nature of their business and the need to restart operations immediately, the company did not have many options but to pay the total of $5 million USD. Unfortunately, ransomware attacks are global and are increasing in frequency and scale.</p>
<p data-w-id="52a74a74-8e97-5beb-e216-7e128527b1c0" data-wf-id="[&quot;52a74a74-8e97-5beb-e216-7e128527b1c0&quot;]" data-automation-id="dyn-item-content-input">In Latin America and Mexico, the cases continue to rise.
