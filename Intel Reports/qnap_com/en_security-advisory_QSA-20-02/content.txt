                <div class="qnap-bs-container row g-0 justify-content-center">
                    <div class="col-fhd-9 col-qhd-8">
                        <div>
                            <h3>Summary</h3>
<p>The eCh0raix ransomware (MR1904) has been reported to target QNAP NAS devices and exploit <a href="https://www.qnap.com/en/security-advisory/nas-201911-25">certain vulnerabilities</a> in earlier versions of QTS and Photo Station. </p>
<p> </p>
<p>We have already fixed these issues in recent releases since September 2019.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>QTS:</p>
<ul>
  <li>QTS 4.4.1: build 20190918 and later</li>
  <li>QTS 4.3.6: build 20190919 and later</li>
</ul>
<p>Photo Station:</p>
<ul>
  <li>QTS 4.4.1: Photo Station 6.0.3 and later</li>
  <li>QTS 4.3.4 - QTS 4.4.0: Photo Station 5.7.10 and later</li>
  <li>QTS 4.3.0 - QTS 4.3.3: Photo Station 5.4.9 and later</li>
  <li>QTS 4.2.6: Photo Station 5.2.11 and later</li>
</ul>
<p> </p>
<h3>Recommendation</h3>
<p>To secure your device and to protect your data from ransomware attacks and unauthorized usage, we strongly recommend updating QTS and Photo Station to their latest versions.</p>
<p> </p>
<h3>Important: </h3>
<p>Regardless of your current QTS version, QNAP strongly recommends updating your QTS to the latest available version for your NAS model to ensure that your device can benefit from vulnerability fixes. You can check the <a href="https://www.qnap.com/en/product/eol.php">product support status</a> of your NAS model.</p>
<p> </p>
<h3>Installing the QTS Update</h3>
<ol>
  <li>Log on to QTS as administrator.</li>
  <li>Go to <strong>Control Panel</strong> > <strong>System</strong> ><strong> Firmware Update</strong>.</li>
  <li>Under <strong>Live Update</strong>, click <strong>Check for Update</strong>.
        <p>QTS downloads and installs the latest available update.</p></li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Tip: </strong>You can also download the update from the QNAP website. Go to <strong>Support</strong> ><strong> Download Center</strong> and then perform a manual update for your specific device.</p>
<p> </p>
<h3>Updating Photo Station</h3>
<ol>
        <li>Log on to QTS as administrator.</li>
        <li>Open the <strong>App Center</strong>, and then click <img src="https://www.qnap.com/i/_upload/support/images/magnifier.png" style="width: 18px;">.<br>
          A search box appears.</li>
        <li>Type “Photo Station”, and then press <strong>ENTER</strong>.<br>
          The Photo Station  application appears in the search result list.</li>
        <li>Click <strong>Update</strong>.<br>
          A confirmation message  appears.<br>
          <strong>Note:</strong> The <strong>Update</strong> button  is not available if you are using the latest version.</li>
        <li>Click <strong>OK</strong>.<br>
          The application is  updated.</li>
</ol>
