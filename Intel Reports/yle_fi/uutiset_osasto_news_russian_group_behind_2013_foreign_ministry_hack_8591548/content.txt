The hackers have however embedded malware in the site concerned.</p><p class="sc-kiMgGE sc-bhgfbr eQvHnw hPpQcT yle__article__paragraph">Third, when the employee visits the site, the malware ends up on his or her computer.</p><p class="sc-kiMgGE sc-bhgfbr eQvHnw hPpQcT yle__article__paragraph">That allows in the attacker, as happened to the Finnish Foreign Ministry in 2013.
